Bankruptcy Notice | Gemtrade LLC
Bankruptcy Proceedings Announcement
We hereby give notice that, as a consequence of the cessation of our
            services effective May 31, 2023, we were rendered insolvent and are
            presently subject to bankruptcy proceedings. Said proceedings are
            being conducted in accordance with the laws of St. Vincent and the
            Grenadines, pursuant to Section 66 of Chapter 151 of the Limited
            Liability Companies Act.
As such proceedings are already in progress, we are unable to
            correspond individually with creditors at this time. Upon completion
            of the assessment and compilation of our assets, including
            outstanding accounts receivable, the appointed liquidator shall
            communicate with creditors by electronic mail.
Notice of the timetable for such communications shall be published
            on this website in due course. We respectfully request that all
            concerned parties await the next announcement.
